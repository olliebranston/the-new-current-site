About

Why this site exists

Go to About

The New Current is a free website about the energy transition, built in public as both a publication and a coding project. It brings together thought pieces, brain dumps, live data, and selective reporting to help make sense of one of the most important and complicated shifts taking place in the world today.

The transition reaches across markets, technologies, policy, and society, and its pace can make it difficult to see what really matters. This site exists to help with that: to think clearly, explain what is happening, and combine commentary with evidence.

Over time, it will continue to grow into a more useful resource for curious newcomers, informed generalists, and people already embedded in the space.
